About Dhyuti

Mission Statement

Dhyuti is an open science and community initiative focused on applying AI to solve hard problems in science and engineering. It aims to bring together individuals working on AI for science and engineering, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our Community

Dhyuti is a community-focused and open science-focused initiative, similar to Hugging Face or Linux for AI in science and engineering. We're about collaboration, knowledge sharing, and applying AI to drive progress in science and engineering – all on a non-profit, non-commercial basis.

Let us all learn, and grow together and solve hard problems. If Dhyuti is able to enable you in that process in even the slightest possible way, our job is well done! If we are able to excite you back into big science and big engineering problems, our job is well done! 😊
